Alex Chamberlain

Advisor

Alex Chamberlain has been involved in the process of software development in various capacities for almost twenty years, with a special interest in solving real-world problems by applying a thoughtful, user-centered approach to software design. He has worked as a software engineer, business systems analyst, software tester, and college teacher of computer programming. Ed Snajder

Advisor

  • Linked-in: Ed Snajder
  • O'Reilly Webcasts: Ed Snajder

Ed is the Database Administrator at Jive Software. He has been administering databases and analyzing data and for over 10 years, and today finds himself working with PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQL Server and Oracle relational database engines, as well as HBase, Pig and other distributed technologies. He works on query and data structure design, performance optimization, systems configuration and troubleshooting for both internal and on-premises systems.

When not having fun with databases, Ed is an aspiring hacker, with a self-built 3D printer, a couple of Raspberry Pis, and several mostly finished Arduino projects. An avid Portland tech community supporter, Ed has spoken at and participated in the PostgreSQL User Group, OSCON, Portland Code Camp, SQL Saturday and the Oregon SQL Developers' Group.

Lee Mendelsohn

Advisor

Lee joined Cinder in January of 2015, bringing more than 15 years of experience in business development and sales to her role as Director of Business Development. Recognized many times over as a “top producer,” Lee has contributed her business procurement, training, marketing and management skills to the success of companies including Infocus, ComputerTech and others in the technology, creative services and communications industries. Lee holds a B.A. in Business Administration from the State University of New York at Buffalo, knowledge that she couples with a passion for building new relationships. A consummate matchmaker, she is frequently found networking at high tech and business community events, adding to the ever-growing list of resources she taps to bring people and opportunities together.

Lee sits on the Advisory Council at Rosemary Anderson High School as the Silent Auction Chair, a cause she has been supporting for more than five years. In her spare time, you can find her in restaurants around Portland in search of the best hot sauce, at home cuddling with her 19-year-old cat, Mellow, or in nature, capturing the beauty of the Pacific NW with her Nikon camera.
